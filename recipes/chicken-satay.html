Chicken Satay

Description

Satay, or sate in Indonesian spelling, is a Southeast Asian dish of seasoned, skewered and grilled meat, served with a sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken meat
  • Salt and MSG
  • Sweet Ketchup
  • Peanuts
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Small cut the meat, put it in skewers
  2. Grill on charcoal until done
  3. Crush the peanuts and mix it with Salt and MSG, Sweet Kecthup, and a bit of cooking oil until becoming sauce
  4. Eat the chicken with the sauce on top
