Nasi Goreng

Description

Nasi goreng is a Southeast Asian fried rice dish, usually cooked with pieces of meat and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salt and MSG
  • Minced Garlic
  • Minced Shallot
  • Meat and vegetables
  • Other prefered seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat your Wok
  2. Put a bit of cooking oil
  3. If the oil already hot, fry the garlic and shallot
  4. When it smell good, put the rice, meat and vegetables, and other prefered seasoning
  5. Put salt and MSG to fix the taste
  6. Enjoy
